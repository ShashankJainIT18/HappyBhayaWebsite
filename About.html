About the founder

Shubham Jain (founder and CEO) of RBE(Revolution By Education) has created this website (www.sarkarikaramchari.com) to help government job aspirants get in-depth knowledge of the exam they are preparing for. Shubham Jain has himself cleared SSC CGL 2017 (Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level) Exam and selected in Income Tax Department.

What do we offer:

In this website, you will get all the information such as latest updates, vacancies, notifications, work profile videos of different jobs, previous year papers and their video solution, free video classes, etc. related to SSC Exams like SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC CPO, SSC Stenographer, SSC MTS, SSC Selection post, etc. With time, we will try to cover all major government recruitments on this website.
